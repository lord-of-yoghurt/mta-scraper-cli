Planned Work

E Subway Jamaica Center-bound trains skip 75 Av and Briarwood
Late Nights, 11:45 PM to 5 AM, Mon to Fri, Feb 27 - Mar 3
For service to 75 Av, take the E Subway to Union Tpke and transfer to a Manhattan-bound E Subway or F Subway.
For service to Briarwood, take the E Subway to Jamaica-Van Wyck and transfer to a
World Trade Center-bound E Subway.
For service from these stations, take the E Subway or F Subway to Union Tpke or 71 Av and transfer to a
Jamaica Center-bound E Subway.
